1.  Call to Order
-----------------

Board Chair Eric Gerlach acted as chair for the meeting, calling it to order at 1941 EST. The Secretary recorded the minutes. A quorum of directors was present, and the meeting, having been duly convened, was ready to proceed with business.


2.  Approval of the Agenda
--------------------------

The agenda was presented by the Meeting Chair. Robert Gissing asked to add a purchase authorization under New Business. The agenda, with this addition, was approved by the general consent of the Board.

-------------------------------

The Secretary presented meeting minutes for October 12, November 4, December 9, December 23, January 6, January 11, and January 23, for approval. The Board agreed by consensus to approve all of the minutes under a single motion.

4. Report from the President
----------------------------

The President addressed the Board regarding some of his thoughts regarding the coming year. He expressed some concerns regarding ensuring that the membership remains engaged, and targeting opportunities to grow our numbers. There was a brief discussion regarding events that KwartzLab should host this calendar year; that discussion should be continued with the membership.

Alex Williams presented the Board with a visualization of our TON attendance numbers (based on guest book entries). TON attendance is steadily trending upwards. A seasonal arc is also emerging.

There was a brief discussion regarding what additional metric could be pulled from the door database.


5. Report from the Treasurer
----------------------------

The Treasurer presented the Board with a financial update. He indicated that our QuickBooks data has all been restored, and that we are almost caught up on all outstanding deposits. The Treasurer also indicated that a draft of the proposed budget revision will be ready for review within the next couple of weeks.
