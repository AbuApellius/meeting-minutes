Proceedings:
==============

1.  Call to Order
-----------------

The Board Chair, Eric Gerlach, called the meeting to order at 2004 EDT. The Secretary recorded the minutes. A quorum of directors was present, and the meeting, having been duly convened, was ready to proceed with business.


2. Approval of the Agenda
-------------------------

The agenda was presented by the Board Chair, along with 3 amendments: 

* Approval of AGM Minutes 
* Approval of June 2 Board Minutes 
* New business: Legar Studios 

No objections were presented, and the agenda, with these amendments, was approved by the general consensus of the Board.

3. Approval of AGM Minutes
---------------------------------------

The Secretary submitted minutes for the June 4, 2011 Annual General Members' Meeting

Motion: Be it resolved that the submitted AGM minutes be accepted.

So moved by Cedric Puddy.
Seconded by Ben Brown.
In favour: 5
Opposed: 0

Motion carries.

4. Approval of June 2 Board Meeting Minutes
-------------------------------------------

The Secretary submitted minutes for the June 2, 2011 Board of Director's Meeting. They were accepted by the general consensus of the Board, although Jonathan Lamothe objected, with no specific objection.

----------------------------

The Treasurer presented the Board with a current financial statement, which was accepted by general consensus.

The Treasurer further indicated that RIM's Proud2Be donation has not yet been received, although it is expected any week now.

  * $48.57 for a second garbage can & garbage bags

Seconded by Alex Williams.
