3. Mitch Altman's Soldering Workshop
------------------------------------
  
Be it resolved that the Board approves Mitch Altman's soldering workshop, scheduled for Friday August 6, be opened up to the general public.

  So moved by the Ben Brown
  Seconded by the Stephanie Smith
  In favour: 6
  Opposed: 0
  Abstained: 0
  Motion Carries
  
There was a brief discussion regarding the need for additional soldering irons and wire strippers, for the workshop. As the Tool Committee is all ready empowered to make such purchases, the Board does not need to approve anything.
